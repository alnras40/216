Kizio Product Details

Kizio makes a family of mobile apps for those who seek to avoid common allergens and ingredients. Services include the original kizioapp from the business, which scans the barcodes of the services and informs you whether the item in question contains the allergens you have pre-selected. The database of the app includes the vast majority of items in the grocery store and checks them for many different allergens, to less common allergens .
